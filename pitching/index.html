03

CURRENT GAP

Why reactive enforcement is not enough

Non-compliance creates signals before it becomes an enforcement case.

01

Signals are scattered

Filing delays, profile changes, notices, complaints, and enforcement history sit across operational touchpoints.

02

Risk is not continuously profiled

Officers need a ranked queue, not only isolated records or routine compliance reminders.

03

Intervention timing matters

Earlier visibility enables preventive notices, targeted review, education, or case escalation.

04

SOLUTION

The proposed solution

An AI early-warning layer on top of existing SSM data.

Data Registry, filing, MBRS, complaints, enforcement history
AI Risk scoring, anomaly detection, explainability, bias checks
Action Risk queue, reason code, recommended action, audit trail
The system supports officer judgement. It does not replace it.

INNOVATION & USP

What makes this grant-worthy

Four USP points that increase SSM's return from a small pilot.

USP 1

SSM-specific compliance intelligence

Built around filing, MBRS, registry, and enforcement realities instead of a generic AI dashboard.

USP 2

Explainable risk scoring

Every flagged entity includes reason codes so officers can understand why attention is needed.

USP 3

Preventive intervention workflow

Risk signals are converted into notices, review actions, case assignment, and follow-up tracking.

USP 4

Low-cost scalable foundation

RM100,000 creates a validated prototype that SSM can expand after pilot evidence is proven.
